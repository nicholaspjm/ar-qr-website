How it works

  1. QR — the QR code above is generated dynamically and points to ar.html on this same domain, so it always works wherever this site is hosted.
  2. Marker tracking — ar.html uses A-Frame + AR.js to detect the HIRO marker through your phone camera and recover its 3D pose every frame.
  3. Overlay — a 3D scene (the floating poster, glow, label, particles) is rendered anchored to that pose, so it stays locked to the marker as you move your phone around it.
  4. HTTPS — mobile browsers require HTTPS for camera access. GitHub Pages serves this site over HTTPS, which is why it works on your phone.
