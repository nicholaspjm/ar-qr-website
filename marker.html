How it works
  1. The QR code opens an AR page on your phone (camera permission required).
  2. The black HIRO square is a fiducial marker — AR.js detects it through the camera and recovers its 3D pose.
  3. A floating poster is rendered locked to the marker, so it tracks position and rotation in real time.
